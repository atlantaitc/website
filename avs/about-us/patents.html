AVS Patents

 

With over 100 patents in its portfolio, authenticity and innovation are two of the many characteristics LUND International holds close. As is the case with any successful brand, competitors will seek ways to try and replicate others’ success. The LUND International brands are at the forefront of this never-ending battle. Intellectual property rights are essential to creative, forward-thinking and innovative brands such as AMP Research, AVS, Bushwacker, LUND, Roll-N-Lock and more in the LUND International portfolio. That is why LUND International has relentlessly pursued, and will continue to relentlessly pursue, those infringing on its intellectual property rights. LUND International has a successful track record of protecting these assets. Please respect our design efforts by avoiding any activity that may infringe upon our intellectual property rights.

Lund International Holding Company is a leading designer, manufacturer, and marketer of branded automotive accessories for a full assortment of passenger cars, light trucks and Jeep, as well as the heavy truck category. Its products include Ventvisors®, hood shields, Powersteps®, floor mats, fender flares, tonneau covers, storage boxes, nerf bars and running boards, among others. Lund International offers the industry's broadest range of functional body and appearance products that provide customers with ways to customize and personalize their vehicles under the brand names of LUND, AVS, Belmor, AMP Research, Rampage, Bushwacker, Tonno Pro, Stampede, and Roll-N-Lock.
