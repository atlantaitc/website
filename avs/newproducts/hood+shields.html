Hood Protection

Our designers have painstakingly worked to create a series of hood shields to compliment the contours of trucks, SUVs, CUVs and passenger cars.

  • Hood Protection

    At AVS, we have developed a hood protection series to not only match your vehicle profile, but also serve as tough hood, fender and windshield protection against bugs, dirt and debris - keeping the hood looking showroom new. We offer drivers of trucks, SUVs, CUVs, vans and passenger cars a variety of style choices and profiles (high, medium and flush mount) to suit their vehicle and personal design preference.

  • Style Differences

    • Bugflector®
      Medium profile, traditional hood shield

    • Bugflector II®
      Higher profile, full-wrap design hood shield

    • Hoodflector®
      Lower profile, wrap design hood shield

    • Carflector®
      Lower profile, wrap design hood shield (car/suv)

    • Aeroskin™
      Lower profile, flush mount, contour fit hood protection
