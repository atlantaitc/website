About Us

Auto Ventshade has roots going back to 1935 and is the innovator of the vent shade product – a device originally mounted above car windows to keep the rain out and let the fresh air in. Today, AVS manufactures precision engineered functional & stylish products that provide cabin comfort & protection for your vehicle - designed to let the best part of the world in and the worst part out.

Why AVS

Our goal is to provide you with the most innovative, exciting quality automotive accessories you can find! From the creation of the Ventvisor(TM) to a comprehensive series of hood protection styles, chrome accessories and more, all made in USA, AVS gives you superior quality, outstanding customer service and industry leading warranties!

What's New

Our passion, coupled with our continued commitment to quality and making your vehicle better, drives us every day. Our engineers and product managers listen to customer feedback and design new products to provide solutions. We relentlessly pursue new ideas, and the newest materials and manufacturing methods, evolving our products as the vehicle manufacturers change their designs, so you can enjoy a safer, more stylish, and comfortable ride. Count on AVS to keep you 'Ahead of the Curve.'

More
Lund International Holding Company is a leading designer, manufacturer, and marketer of branded automotive accessories for a full assortment of passenger cars, light trucks and Jeep, as well as the heavy truck category. Its products include Ventvisors®, hood shields, Powersteps®, floor mats, fender flares, tonneau covers, storage boxes, nerf bars and running boards, among others. Lund International offers the industry's broadest range of functional body and appearance products that provide customers with ways to customize and personalize their vehicles under the brand names of LUND, AVS, Belmor, AMP Research, Rampage, Bushwacker, Tonno Pro, Stampede, and Roll-N-Lock.
