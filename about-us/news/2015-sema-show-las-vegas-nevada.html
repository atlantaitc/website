2015 SEMA Show - Las Vegas Nevada

November 02, 2015
 

2015 SEMA Show - Las Vegas Nevada

It's that time of the year again the SEMA Show is on, and there will be a host of vehicle's showcasing AVS and LUND products. We're displaying a few trucks of our own, too. Take a look.

2015 Chevrolet Silverado

This great looking Chevy features lots of AVS and LUND accessories for the perfect “Every Day” truck. Parts include:

LUND Textured EX-Extrawide™ Fender Flares
LUND Stainless Steel Latitude Nerf Bar
LUND Bull Bar with LED Light Bar
Lund Genesis™ Elite Tri-Fold Tonneau Cover
Lund In Motion BedXTender HD™ Sport
LUND Aluminum Flush Mount Chest
AVS Bugflector II®
AVS In-Channel Ventvisors®
AVS Headlight and Taillight Covers
AVS Chrome Accessories

2015 Ford F-15

We’ve made the ultimate work truck out of the ’15 Ford F-150 with parts from LUND. This includes:

LUND Textured RX-Rivet Style™ Fender Flares
LUND Textured Aeroskin™
LUND Textured Low-Profile Ventvisors®
LUND Terrain HX Steps™
LUND Rhino Coated Crossbox
LUND Catch-It™ Vinyl Floor Liners
LUND Bull Bar with LED Light Bar
Lund In Motion BedStep®
Lund In Motion BedStep2™
Lund In Motion BedXTender HD™ Max

2015 Toyota Tundra

Our 2015 Toyota Tundra show truck is equipped with a LUND Bull Bar with LED Light Bar, LUND Interceptor™ Hood Shield, LUND Elite In-Channel Ventvisors, and LUND Catch-It™ Vinyl Floor Liners.

Other products include:

AMP Research PowerSteps™
AMP Research BedStep®
AMP Research BedStep2™
Bushwacker Color Code Matching Pocket Style® Fender Flares
TonnoPro Hard Fold Tonneau Cover

2015 Ram 2500

Next up is this 2015 Ram 2500 show truck. It has AVS In-Channel Ventvisor® wind deflectors, LUND Bull Bar with LED Light, LUND Catch-It™ Carpet Floor Coverings, and a LUND Genesis™ Elite Tri-Fold Tonneau Cover. Other parts include

AMP Research PowerStep XL™ power running boards
AMP Research BedStep®
AMP Research BedStep2™
AMP Research BedXTender HD Max™
Bushwacker Max Coverage Pocket Style Fender Flares (NEW)
Bushwacker Trail Armor® for Trucks (NEW)

2015 Jeep Wrangler Unlimited

This mean looking Jeep Wrangler is built as a “utility Jeep” and is equipped with LUND Textured Low-Profile Ventvisors® and a LUND Modular Rock Rail System. Other parts include:

AMP Research BedStep® Trail Series rear step
AMP Research JK Aluminum Alloy Fuel Door (NEW)
Bushwacker Trail Armor®
Rampage Products TrailView Soft Top™ (NEW)
Rampage Products Light Guards
Rampage Products Rear Sport Rack
Rampage Products Recovery Mass Articulation "Stubby" Front Bumper
Rampage Products Rear Recovery Bumper

All of these vehicles feature Dropstar/TIS wheels, BF Goodrich tires, a BDS Suspension system, Edge Products items, MGP Caliper Covers, interior bits by Roadwire, MBRP exhaust systems, Airaid intakes, and a finishes by Meguiar's. (The Jeep has a WARN winch, too).

We're excited to show them off, so be sure to come by booth #31027 (Lund International) to check out these vehicles for yourself if you'll be at the SEMA Show. And while you're there, see all of the new products that we've got—you'll be glad you did!

Lund International Holding Company is a leading designer, manufacturer, and marketer of branded automotive accessories for a full assortment of passenger cars, light trucks and Jeep, as well as the heavy truck category. Its products include Ventvisors®, hood shields, Powersteps®, floor mats, fender flares, tonneau covers, storage boxes, nerf bars and running boards, among others. Lund International offers the industry's broadest range of functional body and appearance products that provide customers with ways to customize and personalize their vehicles under the brand names of LUND, AVS, Belmor, AMP Research, Rampage, Bushwacker, Tonno Pro, Stampede, and Roll-N-Lock.
