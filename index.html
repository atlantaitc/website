Our Brands

  • AMP RESEARCH, in Tustin, CA, invents and manufactures innovation in motion, problem solving products that are precision engineered to fit your vehicle. AMP RESEARCH is the creator & maker of the automated vehicle step and the cargo- managing Bed Extender.

  • AVS, in Lawrenceville, GA, is the creator of the Vent Shade – a device mounted above car windows to keep the rain out and let the fresh air in. From that invention, AVS developed the VentVisor™ side window deflector, a full line of hood protection and more

  • BELMOR, Because You Drive Heavy Duty. Since 1922, BELMOR has manufactured heavy-duty truck accessories, including ventvisors, hood protection, bug screens, and more, to help protect & style your ‘big rig’ and make your long haul more comfortable and safer.

  • American made for 50+ years, Bushwacker designs and manufactures precision-engineered premium products that provide protection & style for your vehicle. Bushwacker created the fender flare and offers the widest variety for the most vehicles in the market.

  • Located in Buford, GA, LUND designs and manufactures the broadest range of stylish, high performance protective automotive accessories of any company in its industry! Products include running boards, tonneau covers, floor coverings, storage boxes and more.

  • Located in Southern California, offering products designed by Jeep owners like you, Rampage Products provides a comprehensive line of innovative & stylish accessories to protect & personalize your Jeep for all of life's adventures.

  • Do Head Turn When You Drive By? Lafayette, Indiana-based RoadWorks Manufacturing designs, engineers and produces USA-made high quality stainless accessories for heavy trucks. From front to back, RoadWorks accessories will turn heads everywhere you travel.

  • Roll-N-Lock Corporation has long been the industry leader in retractable tonneau cover technology and is the birthplace of almost all product innovations within this field.

  • Founded on the idea of designing & manufacturing the highest quality truck accessories from the best known materials, Stampede Automotive Accessories offers a full line of exterior protection accessories with specialty coatings to make your vehicle yours.

  • Tonno Pro designs & manufactures premium vinyl tonneau covers. From their patented designs to the quick and easy no drill installations, and industry leading warranty, Tonno Pro tonneau covers are high quality, extremely versatile and highly affordable.

Our History

Lund International has a history of leading the industry in meeting customer needs for automotive accessories.

  • 1935

    Auto Ventshade established

    in Chamblee, GA. Acquired by Lund International in 1998, AVS was built around the innovation of the Ventshade, a device mounted above car windows to keep out rain when the window is open. AVS now offers window shades, hood shields, light covers, and more.

  • 1965

    LUND founded

    by Allan W. Lund, an entrepreneur skilled in designing fiberglass products. Starting with sun visors, then cab spoilers, Lund soon offered 20 appearance products for light trucks, including bug shields, fender extensions, grille inserts, running boards.

  • 1997

    Belmor

    acquired by LUND International

  • 2013

    AMP Research
