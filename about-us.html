About Lund International

 

Lund International designs, manufactures and markets branded automotive accessories primarily for light and heavy trucks, sport utility vehicles (SUVs), crossover utility vehicles (CUVs), vans and passenger cars and serves a diversified distribution network including warehouse distributors, automotive parts retailers, farm and agriculture retailers, home centers, online retailers and original equipment manufacturers (OEMs).

Founded in 1965, and with roots dating back to the 1920s, Lund International started in Anoka, Minnesota with a mission: To lead the industry in meeting customer needs for innovative automotive accessories and with ease when it came to doing business. Lund International is now one of world's leading automotive accessories providers offering functional, high-performance, protective and stylish products from the industry's most recognized brand names including: AMP Research, AVS, BELMOR, Bushwacker, LUND, Rampage Products, Roll-N-Lock, Stampede Automotive Accessories and Tonno Pro. These brands continually set industry standards for design, engineering and innovation. Located in Buford, GA since 2002, Lund International’s global headquarters and distribution warehouse operates out of a 541,000 sq. ft. facility with convenient access for shipping by both air and land.

With over 30 categories of products, Lund International brands offer ventvisors/side window deflectors, hood protection, nerf bars and running boards, fender flares, tonneau covers, storage boxes and toolboxes, liquid transfer tanks, premium floor liners, light covers, chrome, truck bed and Jeep accessories, and cargo management solutions. Lund International brands offer more options and vehicle application coverage than any other brand on the market today. Each product from every Lund International brand provides outstanding value to the consumer and is backed by an industry best warranty and exceptional customer service.

AMP Research: Innovation in Motion

AMP RESEARCH, located in Tustin, CA, is an American design and technology company that invents and manufactures innovation in motion, problem solving products that are precision engineered to fit your vehicle. AMP Research created the automated vehicle step along with the cargo- managing Bed Extender.

AVS: Ahead of the Curve

Auto Ventshade, located in Lawrenceville, GA, has roots going back to 1935 and is the creator of the Vent Shade product – a device mounted above car windows to keep the rain out and let the fresh air in. From that invention, AVS developed the acrylic VentVisor™ side window deflector in a range of styles, and a full line of hood protection options. Rounding out the AVS line of high-quality automotive accessories are light covers, door sill protection, hood scoops and exterior chrome accessories. All AVS products are precision engineered to fit your vehicle and will provide years of cabin comfort, style and protection.

BELMOR, Because You Drive Heavy Duty

Since 1922, BELMOR has manufactured heavy-duty truck accessories to help protect, and style, your ‘big rig’ and make your long haul more comfortable and safer. Products include ventvisors, hood protection, bug screens, winter fronts, rock guards and tank covers. With applications available for all the OE heavy- and medium-duty trucks, rugged, durable, good looking BELMOR products are considered body armor for your truck. BELMOR – the brand truckers prefer.

Bushwacker: The Original

American made for over 50 years, Bushwacker designs and manufactures precision-engineered premium products that provide protection and style for your vehicle. Bushwacker created the fender flare category and is the leader, offering the widest variety of styles, and the most depth in applications, for the most vehicles in the market.

LUND: Premium Style. Lasting Performance.

Located in Buford, GA, LUND designs and manufactures stylish, high performance protective automotive accessories for trucks, SUVs, CUVs, vans and passenger cars. LUND leads the pack when it comes to automotive accessories: it has the broadest offering of any company in the automotive accessory industry! Products include: hood protections, running boards, tonneau covers, floor coverings, exterior accessories, storage boxes, cargo management, bed accessories, hood scoops, truck bed accessories and grille coverings.

Rampage Products: Built for the Trail…Ready for the Road

Located in Southern California, offering products designed by Jeep owners like you, Rampage Products provides a comprehensive line of innovative & stylish accessories to protect & personalize your Jeep for all of life's adventures. Our continued commitment to service, quality and product innovation has established Rampage Products as a significant leader of premium parts and accessories in the specialty Jeep marketplace.

Roll-N-Lock: For Wherever the Road Takes You

Roll-N-Lock Corporation has long been the industry leader in retractable tonneau cover technology and is the birthplace of almost all product innovations within this field. It is this vision, coupled with our continuing commitment to quality, that has made Roll-N-Lock the fastest growing producer of retractable truck bed covers in the world. Roll-N-Lock currently manufactures a range of tonneau covers and other cargo management products that are unrivalled in their reliability, security, and versatility.

Stampede Automotive Accessories: Deflect It, Protect It

Stampede Automotive Accessories was founded on the simple idea of designing and manufacturing the highest quality truck accessories from the best known materials, and delivering them to our customers in a timely manner. Stampede offers a full line of exterior protection accessories for your truck or SUV; practical, yet stylish with specialty coatings to make your vehicle reflect your identity.

Tonno Pro designs & manufactures tonneau covers. From their patented designs to the quick and easy no drill installations, and industry leading warranty, it’s no wonder Tonno Pro stands head and shoulders above the competition when it comes to offering highly affordable, versatile, quality tonneau covers.

History

 

Lund International has a history of leading industry in meeting customer needs for automotive accessories. A fully integrated, tirelessly innovative company, Lund International now serves original equipment manufacturers, warehouse distributors, auto accessory dealers and vehicle owners with state-of-the-art design and manufacturing capabilities, and one of the industry's largest array of product offerings.

Allan W. Lund, an entrepreneur highly skilled in the design of fiberglass products, founded Lundin 1965. Prior to producing accessories for the truck market, Allan Lund was a teacher and insurance salesman. He began producing prototype work for snowmobile, boat, and other recreational vehicle makers and started making sun visors in a Coon Rapids, Minnesota chicken barn in 1974. The Lund SunVisor attached to the cab of a truck or van. By the late 1970s, as light truck sales increased, the company began producing appearance accessories for light trucks.

Lund's second product was a cab spoiler. An authorized dealer program was established to sell products through auto parts stores, auto body and trim stores and car washes. Dealer-expediters, who customize new trucks before they leave the lot, significantly boosted product sales. The company soon expanded to offer 20 additional product lines, including bug shields, fender extensions and grill inserts. One of the company's fastest growing lines from this period was running boards, a product that quickly caught on with women, who found them helpful for getting in and out of trucks.

In the winter of 1997, Lund signed a merger agreement to acquire Deflecta-Shield Corporation of Indianola, Iowa, thereby forming Lund International which manufactured and marketed a portfolio of product from hood shields, running boards, side window deflectors and covers, and visors, to tonneau covers, storage boxes and truck bed protection. The company grew quickly and continued to expand, acquiring Belmor, a manufacturer of specialty accessories for the heavy duty trucking industry, in 1997.

Lund International acquired Auto Ventshade (AVS) in 1998. AVS was established in Chamblee, GA and built the business around the innovation of the Ventshade, a device mounted above car windows to keep out rain when the window is open. The company made a variety of window shades, hood shields, light covers, and other accessories.

To continue the diversification of its portfolio, Lund International acquired Nifty Products, a design and manufacturing company for custom molded floor mats, carpet mats, rear cargo bay trays and trunk liners in 2002. In 2004, Lund International acquired TRENZ, a leading manufacturing a custom grills along with interior and exterior automotive accessories. The next decade brought a flurry of acquisitions. In 2013, Lund International acquired Southern California based AMP Research, the creator of the PowerStep, which offers innovation in motion and is precision engineered to fit your vehicle. That same year, Lund International acquired Prestige, a manufacturer of imported fender flares, and production was moved to the US. The acquisition of Texas based storage box manufacturer, Tradesman, completed 2013’s acquisitions.

In 2014, Lund International acquired the Southern California based Jeep accessories manufacturer, Rampage Products. Early 2015 brought the acquisitions of the Portland based fender flare creator, Bushwacker, and Iowa based Stampede Automotive Accessories, maker of side window deflectors, hood protection and fender flares in basic and specialty finishes. Later in 2015, Lund International acquired tonneau cover manufacturer, Tonno Pro. In early 2016, Roll-N-Lock, the Florida based creator and manufacturer of the most advanced truck bed cover ever made, joined the Lund International portfolio.

Lund International Holding Company is a leading designer, manufacturer, and marketer of branded automotive accessories for a full assortment of passenger cars, light trucks and Jeep, as well as the heavy truck category. Its products include Ventvisors, hood shields, Powersteps, floor mats, fender flares, tonneau covers, storage boxes, nerf bars and running boards, among others. Lund International offers the industry's broadest range of functional body and appearance products that provide customers with ways to customize and personalize their vehicles under the brand names of LUND, AVS, Belmor, AMP Research, Rampage, Bushwacker, Tonno Pro, Stampede, and Roll-N-Lock.
